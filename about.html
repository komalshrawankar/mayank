About Dr. Mayank Sahu

Hindustani Classical Vocalist · PhD · UGC-NET Qualified
Assistant Professor (Music)

Dr. Mayank Sahu is an Indian classical vocalist known for his deep command over raag aesthetics, intricate taan patterns, and the finest nuances of khayal gayaki. His musical depth blends rigorous training with years of academic excellence.

Despite being visually impaired, Dr. Mayank has developed an exceptional auditory sensitivity allowing him to detect and correct even the slightest shift in sur, intonation, or phrasing.

This ability makes his training style highly precise, personalised, and transformative for advanced learners.
